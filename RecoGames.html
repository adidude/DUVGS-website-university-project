Dont Starve together Don't starve together is an 2D Survival game which you can play with your friends. The main task is quite simple survive for long as possible as there are monsters, savage plants and other creepy crawleys that can kill you. And don't go out at night unless you have a Death Wish or whatever you do Don't starve
Tean Fortress 2 Tf2 is a first-person shooter that delivers action packed fun with the ability to play with or against your friends. There are nine playable classes each offering different playstyles allowing for some tactical plays. The video game also has an economy behind it with the ability to trade virtual in game cosmetics for real money.
This is the police In "this is the police" you are the police chief of Freeburg, Jack Boyd, a man who is trying to bank the most cash before he finally retires. The methods he chooses to go about making his income is entirely up to you. Will you help the mafia and earn black money or will you tread the righteous path and earn your income through regular means.
League of Legends League of legends is one of the largest competitive game with a huge player base. Its main aim is to defeat the nexus by destroying towers and battling enemy champions. There are a total of 136 champions in league of legends and each year about 3-5 get released. Each champion has unique abilities and combos to make flashy plays and some champions support other players shielding them or healing them to help them survive.
