Tournament Rules and Regulations

The selection process

A typical tournament will consists of a group stage and knockout stage. The top teams will advance to the knockout stage. The number of matches will depend on the number of players or teams that have entered. However there is a limit, the first handfull of teams or individual players to sign up for the tournament gets priority.

The maximum number of teams are: 16
The maximum number of individual players: 32

For each individual there is a entry fee of £2.00 even if you are in a team. For example a team of 4 will have to pay £8.00 collectively.
The winner will recive a prize and will be added to the hall of fame. As for the rest better luck next time :)

Rules

  1. No cheating that includes third party programs, disrupting other teams/individuals and performance enhancing drugs


  2. No disrespect to players and club leaders


  3. Match fixing any games will result in a permenant bann from all tornaments for a year.


  4. No violence


  5. Breaking these rules will resolve into serious consequences.
