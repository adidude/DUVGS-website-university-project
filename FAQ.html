FAQ

Q1: What do you offer at the DUVGS?
A1: We offer many video games, a variety of consoles and occasionally free food.

Q2: What games do you offer?
A2: Many games such as Fifa, streetfighter, battlefield, a huge variety of Wii games and competitive games and many more…

Q3: Which consoles are available?
A3: All the PlayStation consoles, Xbox 360, Xbox 0ne, Nintendo Wii, Nintendo DS, Nintendo Switch and Pc’s for pc gaming.

Q4: I want more information on the tournaments, where can I find this?
A4: You can find this at the tournament section on the website at: http://duvgs.tk/RandR.htm

Q5: How do I join?
A5: There are many ways to join. One way is to get a form from the Lair at the union when we run one of our sessions on Thursdays from 7pm or, go online and print the signup form and send it to us when we run our sessions. After you have been accepted, you will be invited to the DUVGS Facebook page and you will become an official member.

Q6: Is there any cost to enter?
A6: Yes there is but a small cost of only £2 per session per person. This is to help us get the best equipment to provide the best service we can.

Q7: Can I bring my own games and consoles? Sure! But there is no guarantee that you will get a TV as most of the TV’s will be occupied. However, feel free to bring your own TV’s and anything else that you need.
