Team Fortress 2

Tf2 is a huge game with a diverse set of characters and with each characters having multiple playstyles. Lets take a brief look at each of theme

First character is the scout, he is the fast class in the game but also with the least health, so his aim is to outmaneuver the oppenent or to flank one, he is also the one best suited to completing objectives such as playload as he pushes the cart or or captures the point twice as fast as other characters. He has a a huge arsenal of weapons that can increase his mobility or change his pplaystyle from aggressive to defensive and more. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Scout for a more detailed guide.

Second is the soldier, he is regarded as the more generalist type out of all the other characters as he can deal both long mid and short range attacks and deals direct damage with his rockets. His arsenal of rocket launchers alter his play style to increase his surviability or his support around his teammates. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Soldier for a more detailed guide.

Third is the pyro, he/she excels at close range and with his/her flamethrower, she can deal high amounts of damage per second. She also wears a flame retardant suit that makes her invulnerable to being burned. Pyro's arsenal of flamethrowers allow it to choose whether to go aggressive, or be sneaky and go for people's back. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Pyro for a more detailed guide.

Fourth is the demoman, he excels at mid range and dispersing large groups of players by using his explosives to create chaos. He can use his sticky bomb to create traps or use his grenade launcher to do tactical attacks to take out sentries. He has weapons that allows him to be a melee only class or focus on taking out a particular class. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Demoman for a more detailed guide.

Fifth is the heavy, he can take the most damage and survive out of all the other characters and is equipped with a minigun that allows him to do continuous damge without the need to reload, however he is also oneo f the slowest class in the game. He has weapons that allows him to be a melee only class or change playstyle to either aggressive one or defensive one. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Heavy for a more detailed guide.

Sixth is the engineer, he builds objects that supports the team and allows them to keep the pressure on the enemy, this can either be a sentry or dispenser or teleporter. His sentry deals high ammount of damage is very accurate, his dispenser heals and provide ammo to his teammates and his teleporter allows teammates to travel to frontlines quicker. His arsenal includes different wrenches that allows him to be independent or more sneaky and build a teleporter behind enemy lines. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Engineer for a more detailed guide.

Seventh is the medic, he can heal other teamates and keep them from dying. Once his ubercharge meter is full, he can activate it which will benefit him and his patient ith various effects such as invulnerability. His arsenal of mediguns allow different ubercharges such as increased healing, double damage and more. The medic is generally the one who makes the push happen to break frontlines and attack the enemy. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Medic for a more detailed guide.

Eigth is the sniper, he excels at long range combat an focuses on picking off specific targets such as the medic and recon ahead to provide information to his teamates. His arsenal includes different sniper rifles that allow him to be supportive or aggressive or better at winning sniper duels. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Sniper for a more detailed guide.

The last class is the spy, he excels at going behind enemy lines and destroying their defense from inside out. He has an instant kill ability where if he stabs someone in the back, it kills them instantly. He also has a sapper built to disable and then eventually destroy it. He can disguise and an enemy or go invisible to help sneak into enemy territory. click on this link https://wiki.teamfortress.com/wiki/Spy for a more detailed guide.
